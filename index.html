Bluetooth Controlled Motorized Skateboard

Progess:I have ordered the deck and the special 83mm wheels to attach the motors to. The wheels need to be 83mm in order to secure the motor and gears to the wheels without the hardware being to close to the ground and running the risk of being destroyed. So unless you can buy a skateboard with 83mm wheels premade it is cheaper to buy a seperate deck and attatch the wheels afterward. This project is in progress, check back soon for more updates!
